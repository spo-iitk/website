## Insights on the Selection Process
There were 2 rounds, in the first round there were mix of Data Science and Business Analystquantitative type questions to reach a decision.In the 2 nd round there were questions based on SQL and some HR questions and I had toconvince them why I wouldn’t do MBA and choose Barclays. There were some situationbased questions. There were some casual questions as well.Other than that they asked about my skills but didn’t ask questions related to it.There were questions on my internship as well and they asked me why didn’t get a PPO.
