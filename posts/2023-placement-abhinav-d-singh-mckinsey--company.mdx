## Insights on the Selection Process
- Resume shortlist- Test (ask you to play games)- Buddy interview - 3 rounds: guesstimate, 2 case rounds (case revolves around resume)

##  Preparation Resources

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
- All processes are evaluative, be cautioned, be polite and do your best regardless of whether they are evaluative or not- In case your company asks for other shortlists and preference orders, refrain from showingyour inclination or preference towards other companies, instead try reasoning out why you are agood fit in this company rather than others.
