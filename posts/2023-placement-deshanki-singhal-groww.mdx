## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
Before interview :- Prepare resume points (especially highlighted) well- Practice cases as much as you can- Don’t mug up things, go for concepts- Prepare basic repetitive questions beforehand.- Research a bit about company before interview.During Interview –- Be confident and composed. Try to enjoy the interview process- Have a two-way convo with the interviewer rather than a QnA.- Take time but think out loud your solutions.- Ask the interviewer some check-in questions, keep giving mini summaries in longanswers and cases in order to keep the interview interactive.
